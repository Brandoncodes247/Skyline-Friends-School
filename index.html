About Our School

Discover what makes Skyline Friends School Kisii special

Premier Education in Kisii

Skyline Friends School Kisii is a premier educational institution located in the heart of Kisii town, at the Getare junction along Jogoo Road. With a strong focus on student-centered learning and the development of critical thinking skills, we provide a nurturing environment for students to grow and thrive.

Our school owns state-of-the-art facilities, including well-equipped classrooms, a spacious library and modern science and technology labs. We place a strong emphasis on character development, encouraging students to embrace values such as respect, honesty, and responsibility.
